Important info

Where to catch

Main Bus Stop (#1) at Thomson Bay Settlement, near the Visitor Centre & Jetty.

Tickets

Day pass (~$30 adult / $15 child) – unlimited hop-on hop-off.

Frequency

Every 20–30 minutes, daily from early morning to late afternoon.

Flexibility

You can cycle, walk, or bus-hop between beaches. Many families mix bus + short walks.

Best strategy

Use the bus for farther beaches (West End, Strickland, Armstrong) and walk/cycle to closer ones (The Basin, Pinky, Geordie/Longreach).

Distances are approximate and schedule may vary. Check with information at the jetty or call the explorer team on +61 8 6270 6060

Beaches of Rottnest

Beaches of Rottnest celebrates the island's most dazzling shores — those glittering stretches of sand that seem, at first glance, the very picture of paradise. And indeed they are… until the wind arrives, turning a tranquil afternoon into a rather undignified duel with flying sand. The secret, of course, is knowing where to go.

That is where we come in. Our rather clever algorithm considers wind speed, direction, and the natural quirks of each beach's topography to provide them a rating out of ten — arming you with the knowledge to select not merely a beach, but the right beach.

Why not temperature, you ask?

Ah, temperature. A most subjective metric, for it is tied to personal taste. What chills one soul invigorates another, and what feels like summer's caress to some may feel like winter's spite to others. Wind, however, is universal in its tyranny: no one delights in a mouthful of sand or seas whipped to froth. That, dear visitor, is the problem we have solved.
